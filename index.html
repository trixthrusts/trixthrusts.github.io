Specifications

It is equipped with a total of 6 nozzles, 2 in the rear of the vehicle and other 2 each side of the vehicle. The energy produced from the combustion isexited from the nozzles which provides the thrust for the vehicle to lift off and maneuvre it. To turn right the thrust from the left side nozzles is increased while it is decreased on the right side nozzles and vice-versa to turn left.It is also equipped with a Transmission Gear equipped with levels 1 2 and 3, but here the levels indicate the height at which the car would fly, 3 being highest.The nozzles are cooled down by using a re-generative cooling and the fuel for the car is H2O.
